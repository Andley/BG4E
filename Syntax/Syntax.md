# 全民希臘文句法 (BG4E-Syntax) 


## Part I 希臘文基礎
八大詞類都從拉丁文而來：
- nomen → noun
	- nomen 意思是「名字」
- pronomen→ pronoun
- verbum → verb
- adverbium→ adverb
- participium → participle
- praepositio→ preposition
- conjunctio → conjunction
- interjectio→ interjection
